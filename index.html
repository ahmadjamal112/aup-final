About Dr. Abbas

Educator, Researcher & Innovator

Dr. Arbab Waseem Abbas is a dedicated Lecturer at ICS/IT, University of Agriculture Peshawar with a PhD from UET Peshawar. He teaches at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, has produced 16 MS scholars (5 gold medallists), and supervised 30+ BS projects. He also developed Pakistan's 1st official online Ideas Bank for the Directorate of Science and Technology (DoST).
